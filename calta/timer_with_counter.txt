Name: Timer with Counter 計時器加記數器
Mode: Comp 
Memory: 87

Important‼️:
Not all calculators time 1 second in 50 loops, calibrate variable A with a clock 
用秒鐘調較A至準確(A預設為50)

Program:
50→A:3600→B: /12
Lbl 0:D÷AB→X:M▫+X▫→Y:?→Y: /34
Y≤0⇒-Y-1→M:X⇒Y=M+X⇒X→Y:1M⁺: /60
Y<1⇒BY→Y:AY-1→Y: /76
For 0→D To Y:Next:Goto 0 /87

Guide:
▫: between (-) and hyp

Usage:
input Y≤0 to set Counter to Y
Y入零或者負數 → 記數器設定為負Y
input Y>0 to time Y seconds
Y入正數 → 計時器計Y秒
