Name: Timer and Stopwatch 計時器加秒錶
Mode: Comp 
Memory: 62

Important‼️:
Not all calculators time 1 second in 50 loops, calibrate variable A with a clock 
用秒鐘調較A至準確(A預設為50)

Program:
50→A:3600→B: /12
Lbl 0:D÷AB▫→Y:?→Y: /27
Y≤0⇒atm→Y:Y<1⇒BY→Y:AY-1→Y: /51
For 0→D To Y:Next:Goto 0 /62

Guide:
▫: between (-) and hyp
atm: in CONST menu (shift 7)

Usage:
input Y≤0 for Stopwatch
Y入零或者負數 → 開始秒錶
input Y>0 to time Y seconds
Y入正數 → 計時器計Y秒
